Available query string values:

from_date
Earliest date to retrieve data from (formatted Y-m-d H:i:s)
end_date
Latest date to retrieve data from (formatted Y-m-d H:i:s)
fb_accounts
Comma-separated list of Facebook account number to retrieve data from
limit
Maximum number of items to retrieve
offset
What index to start retrieving data from (used for paging, coupled with limit)

Date formatting is pretty loose. Something like Y-m-dTH:i:s would work fine.

Examples

Since 2013-10-01T12:00:00

Before 2012-01-01T12:00:00

In the year 2012

In the year 2012, from the Facebook accounts 146585512018715 and 17811601, limit of 10
